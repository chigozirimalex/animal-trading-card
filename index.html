German Shepherd

The German Shepherd also known in Britain as an Alsatian, is a German breed of working dog of medium to large size.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Height: 24-26 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 7-13 years
  • Habitat: Domestic

German Shepherd have a double coat which is close and dence with undercoat. The coat is accepted in two variants: medium and long.
